SERVICES

Advanced, multidisciplinary cancer care — guiding patients through every step of their journey with cutting-edge treatment, compassionate support, and hope.

Cancer remains one of the leading causes of mortality worldwide, yet survival rates continue to improve dramatically with early detection and modern treatment. Our oncology centre combines expert clinical teams, state-of-the-art technology, and personalised care pathways to give every patient the best possible chance of recovery.

About Our Oncology Department

Our Oncology Department is built around a fully integrated multidisciplinary team (MDT) of medical oncologists, radiation oncologists, surgical oncologists, haematologists, pathologists, and specialist oncology nurses. Together, we deliver evidence-based cancer care — from initial diagnosis and staging through active treatment, survivorship, and palliative support — all under one roof.

Cancers We Treat

  • Breast cancer — all stages, including triple-negative and HER2-positive subtypes
  • Lung cancer — non-small cell (NSCLC) and small cell (SCLC)
  • Colorectal and gastrointestinal cancers
  • Haematological malignancies — leukaemia, lymphoma, myeloma
  • Prostate and urological cancers
  • Gynaecological cancers — cervical, ovarian, and endometrial
  • Melanoma and skin cancers
  • Head and neck cancers and thyroid malignancies

Diagnostic & Treatment Tools

PET-CT Scanning

Whole-body metabolic imaging for precise tumour staging, treatment response assessment, and recurrence detection.

Chemotherapy

Evidence-based systemic chemotherapy regimens delivered in a dedicated, monitored day-care infusion unit.

Radiotherapy (IMRT/SBRT)

Intensity-modulated and stereotactic body radiotherapy for precise tumour targeting with minimal collateral damage.

Targeted & Immunotherapy

Molecularly targeted agents and immune checkpoint inhibitors based on comprehensive genomic tumour profiling.

Tumour Biopsy & Pathology

Image-guided core biopsy, liquid biopsy, and full histopathological and molecular analysis for accurate diagnosis.

Palliative & Support Care

Specialist palliative medicine, pain management, and psycho-oncology services integrated throughout the patient journey.

Why Choose Our Oncology Unit

  • Weekly Multidisciplinary Tumour Board: Every new cancer case is reviewed by a full MDT to agree on the optimal, evidence-based treatment strategy.
  • Genomic Tumour Profiling: Next-generation sequencing of tumour tissue to identify targetable mutations and guide personalised therapy selection.
  • Clinical Trials Access: Active participation in phase II and III international oncology trials, offering patients access to novel therapies.
  • Cancer Survivorship Programme: Structured follow-up clinics addressing late treatment effects, psychological well-being, and long-term surveillance.
  • Psycho-Oncology Service: Dedicated oncology psychologists and social workers supporting patients and families throughout diagnosis and treatment.
  • Fertility Preservation: Oncofertility counselling and sperm/egg banking prior to gonadotoxic treatment for eligible patients.
  • Rapid Diagnostic Pathway: Two-week suspected cancer wait target with fast-track imaging, biopsy, and MDT review.
