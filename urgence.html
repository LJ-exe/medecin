CARE 24/7

Round-the-clock emergency medical care for critical and urgent health situations — fast, professional, always ready.

Rapid Emergency Response

Our Emergency Care Unit operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. With a dedicated team of emergency physicians, nurses, and paramedics, we are equipped to handle any medical crisis swiftly and effectively. Our state-of-the-art resuscitation rooms and trauma bays ensure that every patient receives immediate life-saving care upon arrival.

Emergencies We Handle

Our emergency team is trained and equipped to manage all types of urgent and life-threatening conditions, including:

  • Chest pain and suspected heart attacks (myocardial infarction)
  • Stroke and sudden neurological symptoms
  • Severe trauma, fractures, and head injuries
  • Respiratory distress and acute asthma attacks
  • Severe allergic reactions and anaphylaxis
  • High fever, seizures, and loss of consciousness
  • Poisoning, overdose, and toxic ingestion
  • Severe abdominal pain and internal bleeding

Our Emergency Facilities

We are fully equipped to deliver immediate, high-quality care from the moment you arrive:

Resuscitation Rooms

Fully equipped rooms for critical patients requiring immediate life-support intervention.

On-Site Imaging

Instant access to X-ray, CT scan, and ultrasound for rapid diagnosis in emergencies.

Emergency Lab

24/7 laboratory processing blood tests and critical results within minutes.

Ambulance Unit

Mobile emergency units with advanced life support staffed by paramedics.

Cardiac Monitoring

Continuous ECG and vital sign monitoring for all critical patients in the emergency bay.

Specialist On-Call

Immediate access to cardiologists, surgeons, and neurologists when needed.

Why Trust Our Emergency Unit

  • Always Open: Fully operational emergency services 24 hours a day, every day of the year.
  • Triage System: Patients are prioritised by severity so the most critical receive care first.
  • Trained ER Physicians: Board-certified emergency doctors with extensive trauma and critical care experience.
  • Fast-Track Minor Injuries: Dedicated fast-track zone for less severe cases to reduce waiting times.
  • Family Communication: Dedicated staff to keep families informed and supported during emergencies.
  • Seamless ICU Transfer: Direct pipeline to our Intensive Care Unit for patients requiring continued critical care.
  • Multilingual Staff: Our emergency team can communicate in multiple languages to assist all patients.
