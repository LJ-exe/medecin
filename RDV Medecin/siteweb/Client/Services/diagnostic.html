Precision Diagnostics

Our Diagnostics and Laboratory Department is the backbone of accurate medical care. Equipped with the latest automated analysers, high-resolution imaging technology and staffed by experienced laboratory scientists and radiologists, we deliver reliable results that doctors and patients can trust. Whether for routine screening or complex investigations, we provide fast, precise answers.

Tests & Analyses We Offer

Our comprehensive testing menu covers all major medical disciplines:

  • Complete blood count (CBC) and metabolic panels
  • Liver, kidney, and thyroid function tests
  • Lipid profile and cardiovascular risk markers
  • Hormonal assays and fertility testing
  • Microbiology: cultures, sensitivity, and PCR tests
  • Cancer markers and tumour screening
  • Urinalysis and stool analysis
  • Allergy testing and immunology panels

Imaging & Diagnostic Equipment

Our imaging suite uses cutting-edge technology to support accurate and non-invasive diagnosis:

Digital X-Ray

High-resolution digital radiography with instant image processing and minimal radiation exposure.

Ultrasound Imaging

Real-time imaging of organs, blood vessels, and soft tissues for abdominal, obstetric, and vascular studies.

CT Scan

Multi-slice CT imaging for detailed cross-sectional views of the brain, chest, abdomen, and pelvis.

MRI

High-field MRI for detailed imaging of the brain, spine, joints, and soft tissues without radiation.

Pathology & Biopsy

Tissue analysis and histopathology for cancer diagnosis and inflammatory conditions.

Genetic Testing

DNA-based testing for hereditary diseases, carrier screening, and prenatal diagnostics.
