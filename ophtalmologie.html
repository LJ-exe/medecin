Ophthalmology

EYE CARE
SERVICES

Complete eye health care from routine vision tests to advanced retinal surgery and laser refractive procedures — all under one roof.

Over 80% of vision impairment is preventable or treatable. Regular eye examinations are essential — many serious conditions such as glaucoma and diabetic retinopathy have no symptoms until significant damage has already occurred.

About Our Eye Care Unit

Our Ophthalmology Department provides comprehensive eye care for patients of all ages, from children with early refractive errors to elderly patients with cataracts and macular degeneration. Staffed by fellowship-trained ophthalmologists and supported by advanced diagnostic technology, we offer the full spectrum of medical and surgical eye care in a modern, patient-centred environment.

Conditions We Treat

  • Cataracts and lens opacities requiring surgical removal
  • Glaucoma (open-angle, angle-closure, and normal tension)
  • Diabetic retinopathy and macular oedema
  • Age-related macular degeneration (AMD)
  • Refractive errors: myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism, and presbyopia
  • Retinal detachment and vitreous disorders
  • Dry eye syndrome and ocular surface disease
  • Strabismus (squint) and amblyopia (lazy eye)

Procedures & Treatments

Cataract Surgery

Phacoemulsification with premium intraocular lens implants — day-case procedure with rapid visual recovery.

Laser Refractive Surgery

LASIK and LASEK procedures to permanently correct short-sightedness, long-sightedness and astigmatism.

Retinal Imaging

OCT and fundus photography for detailed retinal layer analysis and early detection of macular disease.

Intravitreal Injections

Anti-VEGF injections (Avastin, Eylea, Lucentis) for wet AMD and diabetic macular oedema.

Visual Field Testing

Automated perimetry for monitoring glaucoma progression and neurological visual field defects.

Paediatric Eye Care

Child-friendly vision screening, patching therapy for amblyopia, and squint correction surgery.

Why Choose Our Eye Care Unit

  • Sub-Specialty Expertise: Dedicated surgeons for retina, cornea, glaucoma, and oculoplastics.
  • Advanced Imaging Suite: OCT angiography, ultra-widefield retinal imaging, and corneal topography.
  • Premium Lens Options: Full range of monofocal, toric, and multifocal IOLs for cataract surgery.
  • Diabetic Eye Screening: Structured programme for annual diabetic retinopathy screening and early intervention.
  • Glaucoma Monitoring Clinic: Regular visual field and OCT monitoring with prompt treatment escalation.
  • Low Vision Rehabilitation: Support and aids for patients with irreversible visual impairment.
  • Children's Eye Clinic: Specialist paediatric sessions in a child-friendly environment.
