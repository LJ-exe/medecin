Expert Heart Care

Our Cardiology Department offers world-class diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of cardiovascular diseases. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by board-certified cardiologists, we provide personalised care tailored to each patient's unique condition. Whether you need a routine heart check-up or advanced interventional procedures, our team is here for you every step of the way.

Conditions We Treat

Our cardiologists are trained to diagnose and manage a wide spectrum of heart and vascular conditions, including:

  • Coronary artery disease and angina
  • Heart failure and cardiomyopathy
  • Arrhythmias and atrial fibrillation
  • Hypertension (high blood pressure)
  • Valvular heart disease
  • Congenital heart defects
  • Peripheral arterial disease
  • Post-cardiac event rehabilitation

Diagnostic Procedures

We use the latest non-invasive and minimally invasive tools to deliver fast, accurate diagnoses:

Electrocardiogram (ECG)

Records the electrical activity of the heart to detect arrhythmias and ischemia.

Echocardiography

Ultrasound imaging of heart structure and function, including valves and chambers.

Holter Monitor

24–48 hour continuous ECG recording to capture intermittent rhythm abnormalities.

Stress Test (ETT)

Evaluates heart performance under physical exertion to detect coronary artery disease.

Coronary Angiography

X-ray imaging of coronary arteries using contrast dye to identify blockages.

Cardiac Blood Tests

Troponin, BNP, lipid panels and other biomarkers for risk assessment and diagnosis.

Why Choose Our Cardiology Unit

  • Board-Certified Specialists: All cardiologists hold international certifications and years of clinical experience.
  • Advanced Cardiac Imaging: On-site 3D echocardiography, CT angiography and MRI cardiac suite.
  • Multidisciplinary Team: Cardiologists collaborate with cardiac surgeons, dietitians and physiotherapists.
  • Minimally Invasive Procedures: Catheter-based interventions with shorter recovery times.
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation: Structured post-procedure programs to restore heart health and fitness.
  • 24/7 Cardiac Emergency Unit: Dedicated team on call around the clock for acute cardiac events.
  • Preventive Cardiology: Lifestyle counselling and early screening programs for at-risk patients.
