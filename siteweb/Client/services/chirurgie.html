Expert Surgical Care

Our Surgical Department brings together the finest surgical talent and the most advanced operating technology to deliver outstanding patient outcomes. Our operating theatres are fully equipped with laminar airflow systems, robotic-assisted surgical platforms, and real-time imaging capabilities. Every surgical procedure — from minor day-case operations to complex major surgeries — is performed with the highest standards of safety, precision, and care.

Surgical Procedures We Perform

Our surgical team covers a broad spectrum of specialties and procedures, including:

  • Laparoscopic and minimally invasive abdominal surgery
  • Appendectomy, cholecystectomy, and hernia repair
  • Colorectal and gastrointestinal surgery
  • Thyroid and parathyroid surgery
  • Orthopaedic surgery: joint replacement and fracture fixation
  • Vascular surgery and arterial reconstruction
  • Urological surgery: kidney stones, prostate, and bladder procedures
  • Oncological surgery for tumour resection and biopsy

Our Operating Facilities

Our surgical suite is built to meet the highest international standards for safety and precision:

Modern Operating Theatres

4 fully equipped theatres with laminar airflow, advanced lighting, and integrated imaging systems.

Robotic-Assisted Surgery

Precision robotic platforms enabling minimally invasive procedures with enhanced accuracy and faster recovery.

Anaesthesiology Unit

Expert anaesthetists using the latest monitoring technology to ensure patient safety throughout every procedure.

Post-Op Recovery Unit

Dedicated recovery wards with 24/7 nursing care to monitor patients immediately following surgery.

Sterilisation Centre

ISO-certified central sterilisation ensuring all instruments meet the strictest infection-control standards.

Pre-Surgical Assessment

Comprehensive pre-op evaluation including anaesthesia consultation, blood work, and imaging review.

Why Choose Our Surgical Unit

  • Internationally Trained Surgeons: Our team holds fellowships and certifications from leading medical institutions worldwide.
  • Minimally Invasive Approach: We prioritise laparoscopic and keyhole techniques for faster recovery and less scarring.
  • Same-Day Surgery: Many procedures are performed as day cases, allowing patients to return home the same day.
  • Multidisciplinary Planning: Complex cases are reviewed in tumour boards and multidisciplinary team meetings before surgery.
  • Post-Operative Rehabilitation: Physiotherapy and rehabilitation programmes begin immediately after surgery to optimise recovery.
  • Transparent Patient Communication: Surgeons take time to fully explain all risks, alternatives, and expected outcomes before consent.
  • Zero-Tolerance Infection Policy: Strict protocols and monitoring to eliminate hospital-acquired infections in all surgical patients.
