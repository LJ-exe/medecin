Dentistry

DENTISTRY
SERVICES

State-of-the-art dental care for the whole family — combining preventive excellence, restorative precision, and aesthetic expertise for a healthier, confident smile.

Oral health is deeply connected to overall well-being. Untreated dental disease can contribute to cardiovascular conditions, diabetes complications, and respiratory infections. Our dental team provides proactive, technology-driven care to protect both your smile and your systemic health.

About Our Dentistry Department

Our Dentistry Department offers a complete spectrum of dental services under one roof, from routine check-ups and professional cleaning to advanced implantology, orthodontics, and oral surgery. Our experienced dentists use the latest digital imaging, laser technology, and biomimetic materials to deliver precise, comfortable, and long-lasting results for patients of all ages.

Conditions We Treat

  • Dental caries (tooth decay) and cavities at all stages
  • Periodontal (gum) disease — gingivitis and periodontitis
  • Tooth loss and edentulism requiring implants or prosthetics
  • Malocclusion, misalignment, and bite disorders
  • Temporomandibular joint (TMJ) disorder and jaw pain
  • Dental abscesses and acute odontogenic infections
  • Oral cancer and suspicious mucosal lesions
  • Dental erosion due to acid reflux or dietary habits

Diagnostic & Treatment Tools

Digital X-Rays & CBCT

Low-radiation digital radiography and 3D cone-beam CT for accurate diagnosis of bone, roots, and implant planning.

Dental Implants

Titanium implant placement with computer-guided surgery for permanent, natural-looking tooth replacement.

Orthodontics & Aligners

Traditional braces and clear aligner systems (Invisalign) for discreet, effective teeth straightening.

Teeth Whitening

In-office professional bleaching and custom take-home trays for safe, dramatic whitening results.

Laser Dentistry

Minimally invasive laser treatments for gum reshaping, cavity preparation, and soft-tissue procedures.

Paediatric Dentistry

Child-friendly dental care including fissure sealants, fluoride treatments, and early orthodontic assessment.

Why Choose Our Dentistry Unit

  • Preventive Dental Programme: Personalised recall schedules, dietary counselling, and professional hygiene sessions to prevent disease before it starts.
  • Same-Day Dental Emergencies: Priority slots for acute toothache, broken teeth, and dental trauma — no prolonged waiting.
  • Cosmetic Smile Design: Comprehensive aesthetic consultations combining veneers, bonding, whitening, and gum contouring for a complete smile makeover.
  • Full-Arch Implant Rehabilitation: All-on-4 and All-on-6 implant solutions for patients with severe tooth loss, restoring function and aesthetics in minimal visits.
  • Sedation Dentistry: Nitrous oxide and oral sedation options for anxious patients, ensuring a calm and comfortable experience.
  • Digital Smile Preview: 3D smile simulation technology so patients can visualise their results before treatment begins.
  • Oral Cancer Screening: Routine VELscope fluorescence screening at every check-up for early detection of mucosal abnormalities.
